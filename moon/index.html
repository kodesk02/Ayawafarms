Major Products

Broilers

Ayawa farm raises live broilers for sale feeding them and getting weights up to 3kg,They are sold as live birds and can also be bought as frozen chicken after going through processing at our NAFDAC approved and industry standard processing lab. Thus birthing Ayawa Oga Chicken.

Eggs Supply

Ayawa farm is a growing supplier of fresh eggs in Nigeria providing a steady supply of eggs to wholesalers, restaurants ,hotels schools in the area. Our layers are well fed by a nutritious mix formula that allows for maximum production from our birds.

Cattle rearing

Ayawa farm, using artificial insemination in cattle rearing has helped the ranch to fertilise cows when they were detected to be in their heat period this increases the probability of getting a successful pregnancy. These cattle now grow up to produce milk and meat for you through Ayawa farms
